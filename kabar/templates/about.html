About Garam Kabar


Garam Kabar is a community ranked India news site. Users submit websites and blogs with India news and information from all around the web. Top posts ranked by the community reach the front page. GK is a Reddit / Hacker News clone. GaramKabar.com is a free, community run, open source website. Source code of the website is hosted at github. Contributions to the website are open to all. If you are interested please join us by subscribing to the Garamkabar Google group.


Planned features


* Disqus commenting system support
* Flag/warn incorrect submissions
* Ask the community posts
* Users profile page
